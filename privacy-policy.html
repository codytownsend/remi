1. Information We Collect

When you communicate with us via SMS, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Message content and timestamp
  • Information obtained from public HUD records

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Communicate with you about HUD properties and related real estate matters
  • Respond to your inquiries and questions

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process

4. Data Storage and Security

Your message history is stored locally in our secure system. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages
  • Access: Request access to your message history
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your conversation history

6. Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan. We do not charge for messages sent or received.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our SMS service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
