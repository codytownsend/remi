NSPIRE (National Standards for the Physical Inspection of Real Estate) is the updated inspection model from HUD, replacing the traditional REAC inspection process. NSPIRE places a greater emphasis on health, safety, and functional aspects of properties, with more frequent and thorough inspections. This new model also introduces higher standards for unit interiors and common areas, requiring property owners to stay vigilant in maintaining compliance. At REMI Services, we specialize in helping property managers and owners understand these changes, ensuring their properties are prepared for NSPIRE inspections.

Our inspectors provide a detailed report, complete with photographs, notes, and recommendations for corrective actions. This proactive approach helps you maintain compliance with HUD’s updated standards and improve your property’s inspection score.
